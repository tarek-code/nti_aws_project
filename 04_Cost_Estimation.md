## ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Excluded Costs:** This estimate excludes EC2 instances, RDS, EBS volumes, and data transfer between regions
- **Sandbox Limits:** Keep ASG desired capacity low to stay within 9 instance limit
- **Email Confirmation:** Don't forget to confirm SNS email subscriptions (no cost impact)
- **Monitoring:** Set up billing alerts to track actual costs
- **Route 53 Alias Records:** Using Alias records means DNS queries are free (no query charges)
- **CloudFront Caching:** Effective caching can significantly reduce data transfer costs
